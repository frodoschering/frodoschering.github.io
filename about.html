ManyOne is an initiative by Klaas Nienhuis and Frodo Schering that focuses on making complex products, processes and data accessible and engaging. By using modern technology we help organizations create thoughtful experiences for their audience and guide them to a focused outcome. We are all independent professionals which enables us to assemble the best team for the task at hand. Besides doing commercial work ManyOne is also a place where experts from different fields share their knowledge, experiment and innovate.
